1. Information We Collect

We do not operate a standalone website that collects personal data. Our primary presence is on third-party social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). Any data collected through those platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

If you contact us directly via email, we may collect:

Any information you provide directly to us is used solely to:

We use official APIs provided by TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube to publish content to our social media accounts. Product purchases are processed through Amway's official website and systems. Marley's Wellness does not handle, store, or process payment information. Amway's privacy policy governs any data collected during a purchase.

6. Cookies

We do not operate a website that sets cookies. Third-party platforms we post on may use cookies per their own policies.

7. Children's Privacy

Our content is intended for adults 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Data Retention

If you contact us via email, we retain your message only as long as necessary to respond and resolve your inquiry.
